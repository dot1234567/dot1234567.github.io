关于绘本: SIXTEENERS希望以传统童话故事白雪公主为载体，以特别新颖的视角改编创作绘本。 全新的故事中白雪公主成为了一位智慧勇敢的侦探，帮助王国的人们解决问题。公主与王子的故事也不再是以大婚收尾，而是相伴扶持继续探险。 书中的的白雪公主不再是单纯善良没有自保能力的少女，而是机敏果敢的探险者，彰显女性力量，削弱女性社会以及性格刻板印象。

SIXTEENERS wanted to create a picture book based on the traditional fairy tale of Snow White, adapted from a particularly original perspective. In the new story, Snow White becomes a wise and brave detective who helps the people of the kingdom to solve their problems. The story of the Princess and the Prince no longer ends with a big wedding, but with the support of each other to continue the adventure. Instead of being a young girl with no ability to defend herself, Snow White is a bold and resourceful explorer, highlighting the power of women and undermining social and character stereotypes of women.
